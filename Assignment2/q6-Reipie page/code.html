Ingrediants

Ingrediants For the suace
  • Butter – 2 tablespoons
  • All-purpose flour – 2 tablespoons
  • Milk – 2 cups, preferably warm
  • Grated cheese (optional, like Parmesan or Cheddar) – ½ cup
  • Salt – to taste
  • Pepper powder – ½ teaspoon
  • Garlic (minced or finely chopped) – 2-3 cloves
  • Nutmeg powder (optional) – a pinch
  • Italian seasoning or mixed herbs – 1 teaspoon
  • Chili flakes (optional) – ½ teaspoon
Ingrediants for the sauce
  • Pasta (e.g., penne, fusilli, or macaroni) – 2 cups
  • Water – for boiling
  • Salt – for boiling pasta
  • Olive oil – 1 teaspoon (optional, to prevent sticking)
Vegetables
  • Bell peppers (diced or julienned) – ½ cup
  • Mushrooms (sliced) – ½ cup
  • Broccoli (blanched) – ½ cup
  • Sweet corn – ¼ cup
  • Spinach (chopped, optional) – ¼ cup
Garnish
  • Fresh parsley or coriander (chopped) – for garnish
  • Extra grated cheese – for topping (optional)

Tips/Steps

  1. Boil the Pasta
    2. Prepare the White Sauce
  2. Melt butter
  3. Add flour
  4. Gradually pour in warm milk
  5. Cook until the sauce thickens
  6. Cook the Vegetables
  7. Combine Pasta and Sauce
  8. Season and Garnish
  9. Serve Hot

Nutrional facts

  • Calories: ~350-500 kca
  • Carbohydrates: ~40-50g
  • Protein: ~10-15g
